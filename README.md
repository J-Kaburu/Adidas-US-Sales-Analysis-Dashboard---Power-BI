# Adidas-US-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI

## Project Overview
This Power BI project provides a comprehensive analysis of Adidas sales performance across the United States. The goal was to transform raw sales data into actionable business intelligence, focusing on key performance indicators (KPIs) like revenue, profitability, and regional market penetration.

## Key Features
* **Dynamic KPI Cards:** Real-time tracking of Total Sales, Operating Profit, Units Sold, and Average Price.
* **Product Performance Analysis:** A detailed breakdown of revenue by product category, identifying Men's Street Footwear as the top revenue driver.
* **Geospatial Mapping:** Interactive map visualization showing sales distribution across US States and Cities using bubble scaling.
* **Sales Method Breakdown:** Analysis of sales channels (In-store, Online, Outlet) to understand consumer purchasing behavior.
* **Advanced Formatting:** Implemented a professional dark-themed UI with custom Adidas branding.

## Tools Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** For data modeling and visualization.
* **Power Query:** Used for data cleaning (ETL) and standardizing currency/date formats.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Created custom measures for Profit Margin and Sales YTD.
* **Dataset:** Adidas US Sales Dataset (CSV).

## Key Business Insights
* **Top Category:** Street Footwear significantly outperforms Athletic Footwear, suggesting a strong "lifestyle" brand trend.
* **Profitability:** While sales are high in certain regions, the Operating Margin varies by sales method—Online sales generally showing higher efficiency.
* **Regional Focus:** The Northeast region (specifically New York) remains the primary hub for high-volume transactions.
